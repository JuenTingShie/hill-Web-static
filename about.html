Our Story

According to the statistics of the United Nations University, there is about 41 million tons of global electronic wastes, which worth over NT$1.5 trillion in 2014. Unfortunately, only 6 million tons of electronics been used properly. That’s why our vision is to extend the life-cycle of used electronics. We hope to provide a trading platform for used electronic products, for those who don’t need them anymore, and also for those who want to buy electronics but don’t have enough money to buy a new one.


Hill was established since 2019. To extend the life-cycle of used electronics is the core concept of our company. The online trading of second handed electronics expects to start in January, 2020. We provide price trend, tags, forum and user-friendly consultation to bring convenience to consumers.
